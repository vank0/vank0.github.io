How to use

  1. Set your target reps, press Start set, then place the phone flat on the machine's weight stack (screen up).
  2. Each full up-and-down movement counts as one rep, with a beep.
  3. When you reach the target you'll hear a distinct fanfare; extra reps get a lower tone.
  4. If reps are missed or double-counted, adjust Sensitivity — the graph shows what the sensor sees.
